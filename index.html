About Me

I am a fourth-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University and also the State Key Laboratory of CAD & CG. I am under the supervision of Prof. Xiaofei He and Prof. Deng Cai. My research interests include Large Scale Machine Learning, Convex Optimization and Matrix Analysis.
Previously, I graduated with a bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Zhejiang University, China.
Now, I am a visiting student in the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. And my advisors are Prof. Jieping Ye and Prof. Jie Wang.
